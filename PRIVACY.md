# Privacy Policy for PacificOceanAI

**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

## Overview

PacificOceanAI is a browser extension that provides AI-powered writing assistance for the Overleaf LaTeX editor. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

This extension collects and stores the following data **locally on your device**:

### 1. API Configuration
- User-provided API keys for AI services
- API endpoint URLs
- Model selection preferences

### 2. User Preferences
- UI settings (sidebar width, theme preferences)
- Extension configuration options

### 3. Document Content
- LaTeX content from the Overleaf editor is temporarily processed to provide AI writing suggestions
- Conversation history with the AI assistant

## Data Storage

- **All data is stored locally** in your browser using the Chrome/Edge Storage API
- **No data is transmitted to our servers** - we do not operate any backend servers
- Data remains on your device and under your control
- You can clear all stored data by removing the extension

## Third-Party Services

When using AI features, your text content may be sent to third-party AI service providers based on **your own configuration**:

- OpenAI (api.openai.com)
- Azure OpenAI Service
- Other OpenAI-compatible API endpoints you configure

Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services:
- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- ❌ Sell or transfer your data to third parties
- ❌ Collect analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Store any data on external servers
- ❌ Use your data for advertising purposes
- ❌ Use your data to train AI models

## Data Security

- API keys are stored in browser's secure storage
- All communications with AI services use HTTPS encryption
- No sensitive data is logged or transmitted externally

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your stored data through the extension options
- Delete all stored data by removing the extension
- Control which AI services receive your data through configuration

## Supported Websites

This extension only operates on:
- https://www.overleaf.com
- https://latex.sysu.edu.cn

The extension does not access or collect data from any other websites.

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
